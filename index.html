Steps to Start Management of your
Food Resources

01

Take inventory

Start by taking inventory of all the food supplies you have. This includes food items in your pantry, refrigerator, freezer, and any other storage areas. Make a list of all the items and their expiry dates.

02

Organize your food supplies

Once you have taken inventory, organize your food supplies based on their expiry dates. Place the items that expire soonest at the front of the shelf, and those that expire later towards the back. This will help you use up the items that are close to expiration first.

03

Store food properly

Ensure that you are storing your food supplies properly. For example, keep perishable items like meat, dairy, and eggs in the refrigerator or freezer to prevent spoilage. Store dry goods like grains, pasta, and beans in a cool, dry place.

What is a Food Resource?

Food resources means all commodities and products (simple, mixed, or compound), or complements to such commodities or products, that are capable of being ingested by either human beings or animals, irrespective of other uses to which such commodities or products may be put, at all stages of processing from the raw commodity to the products thereof in vendible form for human or animal consumption.

How do I Manage our Food Resources?

Rotating crops and planting cover crops helps to keep soil healthy. Using fewer chemicals and integrating biological pest control and natural fertilizers can help, as well. Precision agriculture, which uses technology to optimize resource use, can help farmers use less fertilizer, pesticides, water and other inputs.

What is sustainable Food Resources?

Sustainable eating involves selecting foods that are healthy for our bodies and the environment. This means foods that provide a balanced diet for the body but also facilitate the conservation of the environment. These are foods grown and processed in a manner that does not harm the environment or compromise its ability to meet the needs of future generations.

What is our government doing to manage our Food Resources?

The food procured by the Food Corporation of India (FCI) is distributed through government regulated ration shops among the poorer section of the society. This is called the Public Distribution System (PDS). This is the most important step taken by the Government of India towards ensuring food security.
